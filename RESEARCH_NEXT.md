### 1. Elizabeth Jane Sorenson's Mother — Frances G.M. Conner UNCONFIRMED
- **Issue:** Frances G.M. Conner married John Spies Jr. in **1914** in Milwaukee. Elizabeth Jane Spies was born in **1924** — that's a 10-year gap. The 1914 marriage record shows Frances as wife, but Elizabeth's birth is 10 years later. Frances could have been stepmother rather than biological mother.
- **Better theory:** John Spies Jr. may have had an earlier wife (who died or divorced before 1914). Frances may be a stepmother only.
- **Next step:** Search 1920 Milwaukee census for John Spies Jr. household — would show all members including any children from a prior marriage. Also: order or search Wisconsin Vital Records for "Elizabeth Spies" 1924 Milwaukee birth to see mother's name on birth record.
